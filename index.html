The
Rulebook

Strict enforcement. Read carefully. By registering, you and your team agree to all rules below.

  1. R.01All players must be 16+ years of age with valid government ID
  2. R.02Teams must consist of exactly 5 players + 1 optional substitute
  3. R.03Use of cheats, hacks, or exploits results in permanent ban
  4. R.04All matches will be played on the latest patch / current competitive map pool
  5. R.05Each map will be best-of-3, finals best-of-5
  6. R.06Players must join the official Discord server before the qualifier date
  7. R.07Match disputes must be reported within 10 minutes of completion
  8. R.08Ringers and account sharing are strictly prohibited
  9. R.09Toxic behavior, slurs, or harassment leads to immediate disqualification
  10. R.10Prize distribution happens within 30 days post finals via bank transfer
